# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Companies, recruiters, and hiring managers evaluating candidates. Candidates taking asynchronous or live automated interviews. Their context is professional, high-stakes, and evaluation-focused.

## Product Purpose
An enterprise B2B platform for conducting first-round or multiple rounds of interviews. It exists to automate, streamline, and analyze the interview process, allowing companies to evaluate candidates fairly and efficiently at scale.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Clear, Unbiased. The tone should evoke confidence and fairness, acting as a reliable enterprise tool rather than a playful consumer app.

## Anti-references
- No overly playful, gamified, or "bouncy" UI (e.g., Duolingo).
- No dark-pattern high-pressure countdown timers that induce unnecessary anxiety.
- No saturated SaaS cliché "startup cream/sand" backgrounds. 

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness**: The interface must prioritize reading and completing the evaluation/interview without distraction.
- **Expert confidence**: The tool should feel like a reliable, enterprise-grade system.
- **Respect the candidate**: Interviews are stressful; the UI should provide clear affordances, calm interactions, and explicit states to reduce cognitive load.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High priority (WCAG AA). Clear contrast is mandatory since it's an employment tool. Support for screen readers and keyboard navigation is essential for equitable candidate evaluation.
